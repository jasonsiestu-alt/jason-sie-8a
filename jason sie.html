Student Regulation & Core Values

Student Regulation

General rules that applied in Elyon Christian schools:

  • Arrive on time and attend all classes regularly.
  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students.
  • Use English as the primary language in class.
  • Maintain proper school uniform and grooming.
  • Keep classrooms and school grounds clean.
  • Electronic devices only for learning when permitted.
  • No bullying, harassment, or discrimination.
  • Academic honesty — no plagiarism or cheating.
  • Participate in school activities and events.
  • Follow safety rules during sports and labs.

Core Values

  1. Respect the school’s code of conduct and values.
  2. Report issues to a teacher or counselor.
  3. Contribute positively to the school community.
